Location

The Continuing Education Center (CEC) occupies a separate site known as the CEC Mini-Campus, anbd is situated on the Bola-Ige Drive, off Ilawe road on the way to the State Government Secretariat in Ado Ekiti.

Courses Offered

It offers three sessions part-time programme of six semesters (National and Higher National Diploma) in the following programmes:

School Of Business Studies

  • Accountancy (ND & HND(Part Time - Evening))
  • Banking and Finance (ND & HND(Part Time - Evening))
  • Business Administration and Management (ND & HND(Part Time - Evening))
  • Marketing (ND & HND(Part Time - Evening))
  • Office Technology and Managenent (ND & HND(Part Time - Evening))

School of Engineering

  • Agricultural and Bio-Enviromental Engineering (ND)
  • Civil Engineering (ND & HND)
  • Electrical/Electronics Engineering (ND & HND)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ND)
  • Mineral Resources Engineering (ND & HND(Part Time - Evening))

School of Enviromental Studies

  • Building Technology (ND)
  • Quantity Surveying (ND)

School of Science and Computer Studie

  • Computer Studies (ND & HND(Part Time - Evening))
  • Mathematics and Statictics (ND)
  • Science Technology (ND & HND(Part Time - Evening))
  • Food Technology (ND)

In addition the Center offers:

  • One-year Professional certificate and Diploma Programme in Comnputer and Management Studies
  • One-year certificate Programme in Draughtsmanship
  • One-year certificate Programme for Professional Draughtsmanship
