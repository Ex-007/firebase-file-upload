DEPARTMENT OF MATH AND STATISTICS

Background

The Department of Mathematics and Statistics offers Courses leading to the award of

  • National Diploma in Statistics
  • Higher National Diploma in Statistics

These courses are fully accredited by the National Board for Technical Education

Entry Requirements for the National Diploma

Candidate are required to possess at least five Credit passes at not more than tw (2) sittings in the following examinations: WASC/SSCE/NECO, Ordinary level GCE or NABTEB as specified below:

  • English Language and MAthematics
  • Any one (1) of Basic scicence subjects from Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Agric.Science
  • Plus any two (2) of Geography, Further MAthematics, Statistics, Animal Husbandary, Government/Civil Education, Economics/Commerce, ICT/Data Processing/Computer.
