SCIENCE TECHNOLOGY

3.5.1

Objectives

The Department of Marketing is one of the Department in the School of Business Studies. The Department offers National Diploma and Higher National Diploma in Marketing.
The National Diploma Programme in Marketing is aimed at producing diplomates with appropriate theoretical and practical knowledge to efficiently carry out middle level marketing activities in industry Public Service and as entrepreneurs.
The Higher National Diploma is designed to produce diplomates for the managerial position with thorough theoretical and practical knowledge of marketing operations that will enable them proffer solutions to the marketing problems in organizations (profit and non-profit organizations).

Background

The department of science Technology is one of the five department of school of science and computer studies and the largest department in the school. IT commenced academic activities in October 1978 when the polytechnic formally took off in Akure. Both ND and HND programme run by the Department were first accredited by the National Board (NBTE) in may 1982. it is on record that it was the first Department of Science and Technology in ] Nigeria to gain full accreditation for its courses.

The Department primarily nurtures the Basic Science and offers courses leading to:

  1. The award of the National Diploma in scinece Technology, and
  2. the award of the Higher NAtional Diploma in Science Technology with Specialization in:
    • Physics with Electronics
    • Applied Chemistry,
    • Biochemistry,
    • Microbiology

In addition to the basic functions listed above, the department services other schools in the Polytechnic in the Basic Sciences, and is also engaged in the rectification of students' deficiencies to the level required for ND admission into the department in the school of engineering and science & Computer studies through the Pre-National Diploma programme in Science.

Admission/Entry Requirements

National Diploma Programme

(Full-Time and Part-Time)
Applicants must possess five (5) Credit passes in SSCE/NECO/GCE in not more than two sittings
These credits must include:

  1. Mathematics
  2. English Language
  3. Biology/Agricultural Science
    and the following Scinece Subjects
  4. Chemistry
  5. Physics

Higher National Diploma Programme

Higher NAtional Diploma Programme (HND) in Science Technology, Biology/Microbiology, Applied Chemistry, Physics with Electronic and Biochemistry Options.

  1. The applicant must possess the Requirements for National Diploma as listed above
  2. The applicant must possess at least a lower credit level pass in National Diploma in science Technology Programme
  3. The Applicant must possess a minimum of One (1) year post-National Diploma in science Technology Programme
  4. In exceptional cases, National Diploma with pass in the ND Examination with two or more years of cognate experience in his/her specified field may be considered for admission into the HND programme
  5. Candidate offering Applied Chemistry at HND must possess credit in Chemistry at SSCE/NECO/NABTEB.
    Candidate offering Physics/Electronics at HND must possess credit in Physics at SSCE/NECO/NABTEB.
    Candidate offering Biochemistry at HND must possess credit in Chemistry at SSCE/NECO/NABTEB.
    Candidate offering Microbiology at HND must possess credit in Chemistry and Biology at SSCE/NECO/NABTEB
