How To Calculate Your GPA and CGPA on Semester Basis

The CGPA is the cumulative Grade Point Average of all the courses yoou registered for in the polytechnic.

It is also referred to as the grade points divided by the total units of the courses registered

Nigeria Polytechnics make use of the following grading system in calculation of GPA and the awarding of National Diploma (ND) and Higher National Diplomav(HND)

Polytechnics Grading System Table

Mark (%) Letter Grade Grade Point
0 - 39 F 00.00
40 -44 E 2.00
45 - 49 D 2.25
50 - 54 CD 2.50
55 -59 C 2.75
60 -64 BC 3.00
65 - 69 B 3.25
70 - 74 AB 3.50
75 - 100 A 4.00

The Standardized Unified Classification of Diploma is thus:

FINAL CGPA Class of Diploma
3.50 - 4.00 Distinction
3.00 - 3.49 Upper Credit
2.50 - 2.99 Lower Credit
2.00 - 2.49 Pass
0.00 - 1.99 Fail
